Welcome to the Weird Side

Decrypting Cryptids is an independent group of researchers dedicated to studying reports, causes, and possible explanations of cryptid sightings in the United States.

We investigate all reports of cryptids, thought-to-be-extinct species, and unexplained phenomenon in the eastern US.
